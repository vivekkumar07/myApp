About RentBingo.com

RentB ingo is a consolidated rental portal for various products. It is an attempt to organize the rental marketplace in India and promote the idea of renting products to save money and space. Our portal lists all kinds of products like Furniture, Appliances, Adventure Gear, Fancy Dress, Wedding Chairs and Tables etc. that are provided on rent by various vendors across various Indian cities . Consumers can search for products or browse through products of their interest and also find out the vendor that rents out the product in their locality.
