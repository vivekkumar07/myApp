About RentBingo.com

Hello

RentBingo is a consolidated rental portal for various products. It is an attempt to organize the rental marketplace in India and promote the idea of renting products to save money and space. Our portal lists all kinds of products like Furniture, Appliances, Adventure Gear, Fancy Dress, Wedding Chairs and Tables etc that are provided on rent by various vendors across various Indian cities . Consumers can search for products or browse through products of their interest and also find out the vendor that rents out the product in their locality.

Why buy when you can rent!!

Renting of goods is in no way a substitute for buying goods. When RentBingo or anyone else talks about collaborative consumption it essentially means that we should collaboratively consume things which we do not need to own permanently. There are multiple instances when you need not own something and should be happy with renting it for a short period of time.

Furnitures

Rent furnitures when you move in a new home or when expecting guests.

Bikes and cars

When in a new town for few days, or going out for some road trips, ease up your budget.

Camping Gear

Cut down your camping budget by renting out Back packs, sleeping bags,tents and more.

Electronic Appliances

Rent electronic appliances that you would be using only for few months or days.

Cameras

Capture moments you don't wanna miss by renting photography equipments.

Event

Pick dresses for weddings, farewells, interviews, and other event supplies.
